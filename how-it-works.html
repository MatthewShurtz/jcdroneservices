How Does This Work?

Here a simple step by step guide how to inquire about our service and what to expect from us!

  • Fill out the information on the “Request an Estimate” page.
  • JCDS will contact you if additional information is needed.
  • Your estimate is sent to you for your approval.
  • Once your estimate is accepted, we will send you the invoice and forms for your signature.
  • Read and sign all documentation.
  • Send us your 20% deposit via PayPal.
  • Our pilots will then complete the service on the scheduled date or dates.
  • Within 10 business days, we will send you a Portfolio Preview with watermarked versions of your photos/videos.
  • If you have requests for changes to particular photos/videos, and you paid for editing services, we will make those edits for you and re-send the files
  • Once you are happy with your portfolio, complete your payment via PayPal
  • Once payment is received, we will send your final portfolio to you to download via Dropbox.
