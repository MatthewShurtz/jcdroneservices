How Does This Work?

Here a simple step by step guide how to inquire about our service and what to expect from us!

  • Fill out the information on the “Get A Quote” page.
  • JC Drone Services contacts you if additional information is needed.
  • Your quote and forms are sent electronically to you.
  • Read and sign all documentation.
  • Send us your 20% deposit via PayPal.
  • Our pilots complete the service on the scheduled date.
  • Complete your payment via PayPal.
  • Once payment is received, you will have access to your photos/videos.
As always, if you have any questions during this process, please do not hesitate to call or email us. We are here to help!
