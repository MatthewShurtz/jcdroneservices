About Us

We are a family owned and operated business. We founded this business because we wanted to show people how simple it is to see their property from a new and beautiful perspective. JC Drone Services offers a cutting edge photography/videography option for our clients to show the beauty and function of their property with less cost, greater convenience and quicker results than a traditional photographer or manned aircraft can offer. We believe in giving our clients access to high quality photography and videography from a new perspective with excellent customer service.
